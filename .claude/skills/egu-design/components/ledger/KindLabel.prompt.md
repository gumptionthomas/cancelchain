One-line: Names a stake's direction — "support" (green) or "opposition" (red) — in uppercase Righteous.

```jsx
You staked 4 GRIT in <KindLabel kind="support" /> of the Local Library.
<KindLabel kind="opposition">against</KindLabel>
```

Green = commendation, red = grievance. These two colors are reserved for this meaning — never decorative.
