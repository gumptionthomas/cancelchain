GRIT — choosing the “G”

Flat-top bevel locked. Now the letterform, sized up to own the token. The custom-drawn geometric G is my pick — it’s the most unmistakably “G”, it’s ours (no font file to ship, perfectly crisp at any size, even a favicon), and its even strokes suit a struck coin. The rest are standard fonts for comparison.
